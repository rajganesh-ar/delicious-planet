# Sourcing

## Our Approach to Sourcing

We are building a sourcing ecosystem designed for reliability, transparency, and long-term sustainability. From the outset, our objective is to establish a supply network that meets global food safety standards while remaining adaptable to evolving market and environmental conditions.

Our sourcing philosophy is grounded in three priorities:

* **Quality and safety without compromise**
* **Responsible and ethical procurement**
* **Resilient, diversified supply chains**

As a growing organization, we are deliberate in selecting partners who align with these principles and are committed to scaling responsibly alongside us.

---

## Building a Global Supply Network

We are developing a geographically diverse supplier base to ensure continuity of supply and reduce exposure to regional disruptions. Our sourcing model focuses on:

* Direct relationships with producers, cooperatives, and processors
* Strategic partnerships in key agricultural and production regions
* Gradual expansion of supplier networks based on performance and compliance

This phased approach allows us to maintain control over quality while scaling efficiently.

---

## Supplier Standards and Onboarding

All suppliers undergo a structured onboarding and evaluation process. While our network is expanding, our standards are fixed and non-negotiable.

### Food Safety and Quality

We require suppliers to align with internationally recognized food safety systems, including:

* HACCP-based processes
* Compliance with applicable regulatory frameworks
* Product traceability at batch or lot level

Where formal certifications are not yet in place, we work with suppliers toward compliance through defined timelines and audits.

### Regulatory Compliance

Suppliers must adhere to all applicable local and international regulations governing food production, processing, and export. Documentation and transparency are mandatory components of engagement.

### Ethical Practices

We expect suppliers to operate in accordance with fundamental labor and human rights standards:

* No forced or child labor
* Safe and compliant working conditions
* Fair and lawful employment practices

---

## Responsible Sourcing Commitments

Sustainability is integrated into our sourcing decisions from the beginning. While our footprint is still developing, our commitments are clear:

### Environmental Responsibility

* Preference for suppliers implementing sustainable agricultural or production practices
* Focus on reducing waste and improving resource efficiency across the supply chain
* Evaluation of environmental impact as part of supplier selection

### Social Impact

* Engagement with small and mid-sized producers where feasible
* Encouragement of inclusive and equitable business practices within supplier organizations

We are working toward incorporating recognized sustainability certifications as our supplier base expands.

---

## Traceability and Transparency

We are implementing systems to ensure traceability across our supply chain. Our current focus includes:

* Batch-level product tracking
* Supplier documentation and record management
* Foundational digital systems to support end-to-end visibility

As we scale, we plan to enhance these capabilities with more advanced traceability technologies.

---

## Risk Management and Supply Continuity

Given the inherent volatility in global food supply chains, we are embedding risk management into our sourcing model early:

* Multi-supplier strategies for key categories
* Ongoing assessment of supplier reliability and performance
* Monitoring of geopolitical, environmental, and logistical risks

This approach is designed to support consistent supply while maintaining flexibility.

---

## Supplier Partnerships

We view sourcing as a collaborative process rather than a transactional one. Our intent is to build long-term relationships with suppliers who are willing to grow with us.

We support this through:

* Clear performance expectations
* Open communication and feedback loops
* Gradual scaling of engagement based on demonstrated reliability

Where appropriate, we work with suppliers to improve processes and align with required standards.

---

## Governance and Integrity

We are establishing procurement processes that prioritize transparency and accountability:

* A defined supplier code of conduct
* Internal review and approval mechanisms for supplier selection
* Zero tolerance for unethical practices, including bribery or misrepresentation

These structures are designed to strengthen trust across our supply chain as we grow.

---

## Looking Ahead

As we expand, our sourcing strategy will continue to evolve with increased investment in:

* Supplier certification and auditing programs
* Digital traceability systems
* Sustainability measurement and reporting

Our goal is to build a sourcing network that is not only efficient and scalable, but also responsible and future-ready.

---

## Become a Supplier

We are actively engaging with producers and partners who meet our standards and share our long-term vision.

To express interest in working with us, please contact our sourcing team with:

* Company profile and product categories
* Relevant certifications and compliance documentation
* Operational capabilities and geographic coverage
